About

I'm a master student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, advised by Chris H.Q. Ding. Since July 2022, I've been a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia, mentored by Jindong Wang.

My previous research includes sparse coding (especially compressed sensing in image application), computer vision, semi/self-supervised learning and adversarial attack/defense.

My vision is to build machine learning system (especially computer vision system) that id both trustworthy and broadly applicable. Hence, I pay extensive attention to the progress of robustness, interpretability, edge/distributed AI and learning from imperfect data.
